About Us

As lots of stray animals are homeless and they need care and a roof upon them so our website will help to find such animals and other people who wants to foster them can do that with the help of this site. Many of the foster houses are suffering from lack of supplies so we decided to add donation facility where people who are willing to can donate their fund by UPI.

Our Work

Our website is charity based online website where people can adopt stray animals and also can donate their part of love as a fund. People willing to donate and share their love towards such animals can do so by donating their fund on website through UPI or can also donate on site by our reference. This money will directly gets transferred to the needy Animal Foster houses & shelters by which they can use it for taking care of animals like by giving them needful medicines, first aids, nutritious food & other supplements.
