Our Voluteers

The volunteers take out some time out of their busy schedules and come to help out at the animal shelter. We believe this to be a very nobel thing to do as we do not have any money to spare when it comes to running this animal sanctuary. The people who come and work for us, are taught everything to do with the welfare of the animals and that means that they can do all the things our carers can do. This is a very good life skill to have and that means we believe that we are also educating people about animals and birds as they do all the tasks that the carers at our shelter can do. Here you can also find tasks that volunteers do and how to become a volunteer yourself.

Feeding

We teach volunteers how to feed the birds and animals.

Cleansing

We teach volunteers how to clean and look after the birds and animals.

Vet care

We also take veterinary doctors as volunteers for regular medical checkups.

Security

We teach volunteers how to save a bird or animal from any kind of trouble.
